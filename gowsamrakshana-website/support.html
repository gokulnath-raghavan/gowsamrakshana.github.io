Adopt a Cow

By adopting a cow for ₹1,500 per month, you can help provide food, shelter, and medical care for an abandoned cow. As an adopter, you will:

  • Receive photos and updates of your adopted cow
  • Be able to visit your cow at our goshala
  • Have your name displayed at our goshala as a proud adopter
  • Receive the spiritual benefits of cow protection

You can adopt a cow for any duration - one month, several months, or even years. Every month of support helps us continue our mission.
